About the CEO

Leading with Vision, Service & Integrity

Belal Hossain is the Founder & CEO of Al-Insaniyat Overseas (RL-2504), a government-approved recruitment agency based in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

With a strong commitment to excellence, he leads the organization in delivering reliable manpower solutions across GCC and international markets. The company specializes in sourcing, screening, and deploying highly skilled, semi-skilled, and general workforce tailored to client requirements.

Driven by professionalism, compliance, and long-term partnerships, Al-Insaniyat Overseas continues to bridge global employers with quality Bangladeshi talent.
